Sector selection for Weinstein Stage 1→2 breakouts
Each SPDR sector ETF is checked on two Weinstein criteria: (1) its own stage on the weekly chart (30W MA + prior trend), and (2) Mansfield RS vs SPY.

hunt  sector in Stage 2 and RS > 0 — leadership confirmed, tailwind in place. Hunt Stage 1→2 stock breakouts here.
watch  sector in Stage 1 and RS turning up — early rotation, not yet actionable but component leaders may lead the sector out.
avoid  Stage 3, Stage 4, or no RS momentum. Countertrend risk.
Money-flow plot — where each sector is going
Each circle is a sector's current (RS, RS slope); the fading tail is the last 8 weeks. Position is RS vs slope only — color is the Weinstein state, which also uses the sector's own stage (not on the plot). So a circle in the "leading" corner can still be avoid if the sector itself is topping. The list under the plot spells out which is which.
